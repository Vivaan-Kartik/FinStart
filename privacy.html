Privacy Policy

How FinStart collects, uses, and protects your data

Last updated: January 2025

1. Data Controller

FinStart is operated as an educational platform. As the founder is a minor (under 18 years of age), the legal data controller until the founder's 18th birthday is a parent or legal guardian on their behalf. For data inquiries, contact: hello@finstart.de

2. Data We Collect

When you create an account or join our mailing list, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted form — we never see your actual password)
  • Your investor profile quiz responses (stored locally in your browser, not sent to our servers unless you explicitly share them)
  • Basic usage analytics (pages visited, time spent — anonymised and aggregated via standard analytics tools)

We do not collect financial information, payment card details (these are handled directly by Stripe), or sensitive personal data beyond the above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Create and maintain your FinStart account
  • Send you educational newsletters and product updates (only if you consented)
  • Process premium subscription payments (via Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Improve the platform based on anonymised usage patterns

We never sell your data. We never share your data with advertisers. FinStart is ad-free.

4. GDPR Rights (EU/Germany)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority (in Germany: Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@finstart.de

5. Cookies

FinStart uses minimal cookies necessary for the platform to function (such as keeping you logged in). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We may use anonymised analytics cookies — you can opt out of these in your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing for Premium subscriptions. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Mailchimp — email list management for newsletter subscribers. Mailchimp's privacy policy applies to email subscribers.
  • YouTube — embedded videos from our YouTube channel. YouTube's privacy policy applies when viewing embedded videos.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your data is removed within 30 days. Email list data is managed by Mailchimp — you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send you.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: hello@finstart.de
